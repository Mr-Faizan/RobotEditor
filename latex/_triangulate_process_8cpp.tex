\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Implementation of the post processing step to split up all faces with more than three indices into triangles. 

The triangulation algorithm will handle concave or convex polygons. Self-\/intersecting or non-\/planar polygons are not rejected, but they\textquotesingle{}re probably not triangulated correctly.

DEBUG SWITCHES -\/ do not enable any of them in release builds\+:
